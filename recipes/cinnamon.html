Cinnamon Rolls

Recipe

An easy cinnamon rolls recipe to awaken your creativity and invite cosiness to your home on a rainy Sunday afternoon!

Ingredients

  • 300g self-raising flour
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 70g butter
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 130ml milk, plus extra for glazing
