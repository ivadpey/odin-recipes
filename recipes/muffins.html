Banana Muffins

Recipe

A perfect addition to your cosy baking skills, banana muffins add a fresh vibe to your weekend breakfast and go perfectly with any coffee!

Ingredients

  • 200g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • half tsp bicarbonate soda
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 75g melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 125ml buttermilk
